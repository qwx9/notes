# Harley Benton P-style bass kit

## Specs
- 4 string
- diy set, no finish
- bolt-on neck
- rengas body, maple neck, amaranth fretboard
- double action truss rod
- 21 frets, 864mm scale
- 42mm nut
- 1 p-style split coil
- 1 volume 1 tone controls
- chrome hardware, die-cast machine heads
- 0.045-0.105 strings
